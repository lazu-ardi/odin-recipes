Key Lime Cheesecake Recipe

Delicious key lime cheesecake recipe

Ingredients :

  • 11⁄2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 6 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 680 gram of cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon grated lime zest
  • 2⁄3 cup lime juice

Steps :

  1. Combine cookie or graham cracker crumbs with butter or margarine. Press into bottom and partially up sides of 9 inch springform pan, and refrigerate.
  2. In a large bowl, beat with an electric mixer the cream cheese, sugar, lime peel, and cornstarch until smooth and fluffy.
  3. Beat eggs one at a time, blending just until smooth.
  4. Add key lime juice with mixer setting on low.
  5. Finish mixing with hand. Do not over beat or cake will crack during baking. Pour batter into prepared crust.
  6. Bake at 150℃ for 55 to 65 minutes, or until set. To minimize cracking, place a shallow pan half full of hot water on lower rack during baking.
  7. Turn oven off, and let the cheesecake stand in oven 30 minutes with door open at least 4 inches. remove from oven. Refrigerate cake overnight, and up to 3 days
  8. Slice and serve on plate.
