Chicken and Egg Salad

This is delicious chicken and egg salad recipes

List of Ingredients :

  • 2 cups diced cooked chicken
  • 3 hard-boiled eggs, diced
  • 1⁄2 onion, chopped
  • 1 stalk celery, diced
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 pinch salt and cracked black pepper to taste

Steps :

  1. Combine chicken, eggs, onion, celery, mayonnaise, cilantro, parsley, salt, and pepper in a big bowl and stir well
  2. Taste the salt, pepper, and mayonnaise to your liking
  3. Place in the refrigerator to chill about 3 hours
  4. Serve and enjoy
